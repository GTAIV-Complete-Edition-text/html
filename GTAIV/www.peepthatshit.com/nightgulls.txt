The Gulls is a story about prejudice that has a strong environmental message and some freaky mid-air sex scenes.   After a prince falls in love with a seagull queen, a great war breaks out.  Man releases oil slicks and industrial waste into the oceans; the seagulls rip open trash bags, steal bites from sandwiches and shit on the audience.  Will inter-species love prevail?
